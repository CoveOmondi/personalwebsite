Journey Here is my life journey

Birth & Early life

I was born as the firstborn child in my family. I spent my childhood years in Umoja Estate, a lively area located in Nairobi. I attended Unity Primary School for my primary education, where I first discovered my love for languages, which would become a significant part of my life in the future.

High School

I enrolled at St. Peters Mumias, in Western Kenya. Here, I learnt French and enjoyed it immensely. I also developed an interest in spoken English by participating in debates and public speaking competitions. My curiosity for languages led me to explore different cultures and appreciate diversity in the world.

Campus

After high school, I enrolled at Multimedia University of Kenya in 2020 to study Mathematics and Computer Science. I started my tech career by developing websites and creating vlogs and small edits for my friends. In 2021, I landed my first freelance gig as a video editor, which helped me gain valuable experience and exposure.

First Job

In May 2022, I was thrilled to get my first job as an Associate Video Editor and Voiceover Artist for Oracom Group. It was an exciting opportunity to use my skills in a professional setting, and I was eager to learn as much as I could. This led me to become the Lead Motion Graphics Designer.

Career in Data

During my time at university, I developed an interest in data analysis. In late 2021, I joined a global peer mentorship program on Twitter and gained valuable skills in this area. It was fascinating to learn how data can be analyzed to solve real-world problems, and I enjoyed exploring the possibilities of this field.

Data Science & ML

In 2022, I discovered my passion for Machine Learning and created my first data cleaning model for KeOnline, a business database. This experience showed me the power of machine learning and how it can be used to make complex tasks more manageable.
